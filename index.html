» Meet The Authors

Ryan Kissinger is an engineer with a passion for writing and creative endeavors. He enjoys art: both digital and physical, music, and writing, while also applying his experience with engineering in mechatronic projects. He represents his interests with engineering in Autononymous, with the hopes to inspire others to explore its application in the fields of art. Ryan is currently writing The Frostburn Chronicles, a fantasy/science fiction novel slated for release some time in early 2021.
