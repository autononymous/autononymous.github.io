Creator Profile

An insult to the craft since '95

I enjoy making odd thins with words, sometimes pictures, even fewer times sounds. Enjoy Science Fiction, horror, or just anything that justifies thinking the world is a stranger place than it appears.
Likes:
  • Using the whole tube of lip balm before losing it.
  • Hauntings.
  • Music.
Dislikes:
  • The word 'huggles.' Not a more sinister, vile, repugnant word exists in the English language. This word is the word that caused God to abandon us like a teenage mother at the doorstep of cold oblivion.
  • Cockroaches (and I've got loads).
