 About Me 

My name is Savant-Guarde. Born and raised in Colorado, I work as a design engineer by day, but when numbers start to blur together, I turn to fiction for balance.

Writing has been my creative escape for years, with several short stories under my belt. However, my main focus for the past seven years has been Firebrand, along with my newest project, Paragate.

You can find my work in several places, but this website serves as the primary hub for my stories.

 SUPPORT 

Everything you find on this website is free: I'm more concerned with sharing engaging stories with the world than making money from it. But if you want to show your support, you will find a link to my KoFi page below.

As a newly self-publishing writer, such support means the world to me.
