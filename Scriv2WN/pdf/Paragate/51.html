Chapter Fifty-One

Daybreak

01

Katiya reaches the burning district and finds Nhytyx leading the same kind of horror that defined her childhood trauma: except Endogeny is present (Firebrand). For one terrible beat she feels the old paralysis, then starts giving orders and moving anyway.

02

Cody ignores Katiya's command to leave and follows just far enough behind to matter. He makes one small but decisive assist—protecting or pulling clear the person Katiya cannot split herself to save—so she can confront the real threat.

03

Katiya defeats Nhytyx and saves her family in a direct mirror of the scene that once broke her. At dawn, she completes one final public action that proves to the city and to herself that she has become the protector she always wanted to be.
